About Me

I'm a New Orleanean/Cuban who caught the coding bug while studying music in college. After that, I worked for several years in software development as an application tester and support administrator, among other roles. As a bit of a "Jill of all trades," I've also worked as an illustrator, a graphic designer, a web development tester, a copy writer, a copy editor, and a technical writer. Through my experience, I've learned that I thoroughly enjoy working through puzzles and creating beautiful solutions. There are no problems that cannot be solved in a good way.
